Saru-Sichr is a morning star in Pillars of Eternity II: Deadfire.

Description[]

During a period of Aedyran incursion into Er Glanfath, a group of freelance raiders plundered this weapon from an Engwithan ruin. The fercnyng's court was rumored to pay well for such artifacts and the brigands hoped to fetch a good price for the morning star when they returned to the Empire. Unfortunately for them, they were intercepted by a scouting party of the Glanfathan Fangs and charged with violating the sacred site. The evidence against the looters was overwhelming and they were summarily sentenced to death. The Fangs carried out the sentence at the threshold of the ruins, using this morning star as the means of execution. The Aedyran's bodies were left as a warning to other potential despoilers.
